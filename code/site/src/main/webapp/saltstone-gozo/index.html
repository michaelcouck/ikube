The food philosophy

Real food.
No sugar. No shortcuts.

Every meal at Saltstone is built around a ketogenic, high-protein approach. We're not a vegetarian retreat, and we're not apologising for it. Meat, fish, eggs, and quality fats are the foundation — sourced from Gozitan farmers and the Mediterranean sea.

No refined sugar. Grains, bread, pasta and rice are kept to a minimum — not forbidden, but never the focus. Just food that works with your body — reducing inflammation, stabilising energy, and letting you feel what it's like to be genuinely well-fed without the crash.

✓ Local fish & fresh seafood daily
✓ Gozitan grass-fed meat & free-range eggs
✓ Organic olive oil, avocado, nuts & seeds
✓ Seasonal low-carb vegetables & fresh herbs
✓ Bone broths, herbal detox teas & electrolyte drinks
↓ Minimal sugar, grains, bread, pasta & rice — not eliminated, but kept very low
✗ Not vegetarian or vegan — this retreat is meat & fish forward
Breakfast

Eggs your way, smoked fish, avocado, grilled tomatoes, olive oil, black coffee or herbal tea.

Dinner

Slow-roasted Gozitan lamb or beef, charred greens, herb-infused oils, and a clean finish — no dessert, no regrets.

Snacks

Nuts, olives, hard cheese, charcuterie, herbal teas. Nothing processed. Nothing that spikes your blood sugar.

A note on dietary requirements: Our food programme is intentionally keto and meat-forward. If you follow a strict vegetarian or vegan diet, Saltstone may not be the right fit — and we'd rather be honest about that upfront. Pescatarians are very well catered for. If you have specific allergies or intolerances, get in touch and we'll do our best.

The island

Why Gozo?

Gozo is Malta's quieter, greener sister island — a 20-minute ferry from the world, but a different rhythm entirely. The kind of place where you slow down without trying.

The island has a deep organic farming culture, pristine sea, dramatic cliffs, ancient temples, and a pace of life that makes rest feel natural rather than forced.
